Fully Proprietary (Built from Scratch)
Graph-JEPA Architecture is an  adaptation of Meta's V-JEPA 2 for tactical drone defense. Uses Graph Attention Network (GAT) layers instead of standard vision transformers to represent drone swarms as graphs. Includes a State Decoder for "Dream View" visualization (the AI shows you what it's imagining). This does not exist anywhere else.
War Planner Agent is our COA generation engine with doctrine-aware reasoning, AAR learning loop, and V-JEPA integration. The advisor generates plans; the JEPA general war-games them in latent space.
JEPA Knowledge Base creates a neural bridge from OSINT text intelligence → 128-dim context vectors → JEPA encoder injection. Grounds the world model in real-world intelligence. No other C-UAS system does this.
Bayesian Particle Filter Tracker is a custom 100-particle filter with control-mode classification (distinguishes human-piloted vs AI vs pre-programmed threats from heading variance alone).
Swarm Coordination Engine a multi-drone intercept planner with 8 formations, mesh networking, and collaborative converge-from-all-angles targeting.
ELO Adaptive Difficulty a three-tier rating system (Session/Global/AOR) that ensures perpetual AI improvement. This system (borrowed from chess) ensures our AI gets harder the better it gets. Our adversary AI escalates tactics (swarm attacks, pincer movements) as our defender improves. This is a closed-loop self-improvement engine.
Full C2 Stack we have a 109,000+ file OS. Qluu is not a wrapper around someone else's API.
Open-Source Foundations (Industry Standard):
PyTorch (model training), React (UI), Fastify (API), CesiumJS (3D tiles), PostgreSQL (database), Prometheus/Grafana (monitoring). These are tools. The intelligence built on top is ours.
